Chaehwa Yoo is an Assistant Professor of Electrical Engineering at Chungbuk National University, leading the Machine Intelligence and Vision Lab (**MIVLab**) in Cheongju, Korea. She received her Ph.D. and Bachelor of Science in Electronic and Electrical Engineering from Ewha Womans University under the supervision of [Prof. Je-Won Kang](https://eca.xglab.tech/member/index.html). Her academic training also includes completing an Intensive Global Training Program in Artificial Intelligence at Carnegie Mellon University.

Previously, Dr. Yoo was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Ewha Womans University. She was also a Visiting Student Researcher at Harvard University and Massachusetts General Hospital. Her research focuses on understanding the mechanisms of deep learning—with specific emphasis on meta-learning, unsupervised domain adaptation (UDA), and explainable AI (XAI)—and applying these technologies to real-world problems such as medical signal/image processing, image/video understanding, autonomous driving, and defense.
